First: who are the players?

There are 3 parties:

End User
→ the customer who pays (UPI / card / etc)

Partner
→ the business that integrated your APIs (their app/website)

Paycher (you)
→ the platform in the middle using Paywise

Step-by-step money flow (real life)
Step 1: User pays

A user is on partner’s app

Partner calls your API

You create a payment

User pays via Paywise

👉 Money goes from user’s bank → Paywise

Step 2: Where does the money go next?

This is the key point 👇

❌ Money does NOT go directly to the partner’s bank
✅ Money first comes to Paycher’s control account (via Paywise)

Why?

Compliance

Reconciliation

Refunds

Commission calculation

Fraud handling

So at this moment:

Paywise → Paycher (holding money)

Step 3: Partner “earns” money (but doesn’t receive it yet)

Out of the paid amount:

Paycher keeps commission

Remaining amount belongs to partner

Example:

User pays        ₹1000
Paycher fee      ₹30
Partner earning  ₹970


👉 That ₹970 is partner’s money, but it is still inside Paycher.

🔑 THIS is why we need a Partner Wallet
What is Partner Wallet really?

👉 It is NOT real money
👉 It is an internal record (ledger) saying:

“Paycher owes this partner ₹970”

So:

Partner wallet = how much Paycher owes the partner

Not cash

Not bank balance

Just accounting

Think of it like:

Amazon seller balance

Uber driver wallet

Zomato partner earnings

Why not send money instantly to partner bank?

Because:

Users may request refunds

Payments may fail later

Disputes can happen

Partners want batch withdrawals

Banks don’t support millions of micro transfers

So money stays temporarily with Paycher.

Step 4: Settlement (withdrawal)

When partner says:

“Give me my money now”

They create a settlement request.

Settlement means:
👉 Move money from Paycher → partner’s bank

This can be done by:

Manual bank transfer

Same Paywise (if supported)

Any other payout method

That part is outside the wallet logic.

Simple full picture (1 glance)
User → Paywise → Paycher (holds money)
                     |
                     ↓
              Partner Wallet (record)
                     |
            Settlement request
                     |
                     ↓
              Partner Bank Account

Very important clarification
❌ Partner wallet ≠ money stored on website
✅ Partner wallet = internal accounting record

The real money is:

In Paywise / Paycher’s bank

Until settlement happens
